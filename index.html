Community Rules & Waiver
Please read before reserving your slot.
Reservations & Attendance
Confirmed slot required. Please don't come to a game unless your reservation slot is confirmed. Walk-ins without a confirmed slot may not be accommodated.
No cancellations within 4 hours of the game start time. Cancel earlier so someone on the waitlist can take your slot.
No-shows are held accountable. If you reserve and don't show up without canceling, you won't be able to join the next game until an organizer manually approves you.
Your slot is non-transferable. Don't pass your reserved slot to someone else without informing an organizer.
Payment & Punctuality
Settle your fee on or before game day. Repeated unpaid no-shows may affect future reservations.
Be on time. Arrive before start. Late slots may be released to walk-ins after the grace period.
Conduct & Care
Christ-centered sportsmanship. Play with honesty, humility, and respect for fellow players, organizers, referees, and the venue. Aggressive, abusive, or unsafe behavior may lead to removal.
Respect the venue. Follow the host venue's rules and help keep the space clean.
Proper gear. Wear appropriate athletic attire and non-marking court shoes for indoor venues. Bring water.
Health & Liability Waiver
Play at your own risk. I understand that basketball, pickleball, badminton, running, dragonboat, and related activities involve inherent risks of injury.
Release of liability. I voluntarily participate at my own risk and release CCF Makati Sports, its organizers, leaders, and volunteers from any liability for injuries, accidents, hospitalization, or health issues arising from participation in the runs, open plays, or any activity.
Fitness to play. I confirm I am physically fit to participate. I will not play if injured or unwell, will consult a doctor if I have a medical condition, and will stop and inform an organizer if I feel unwell during play.
