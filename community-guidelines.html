Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 15, 2026

CCF Makati Sports is a friendly, faith-rooted sports community. These guidelines keep our games and our app a welcoming place for everyone. They apply to how you treat people on and off the court and to anything you post in the app's Feed. By using the app, you agree to follow them.

Treat everyone with respect

  • Be kind and welcoming, especially to new players.
  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or personal attacks.
  • No hate speech or discrimination based on gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, or any personal characteristic.
  • Disagree gracefully. Keep banter good-natured.

Keep content appropriate

  • No sexual, violent, graphic, or otherwise disturbing content.
  • No content promoting illegal activity, gambling, or substance abuse.
  • No spam, scams, or unsolicited advertising and selling.
  • Post things relevant to our community — games, fellowship, encouragement, announcements.

Photos and privacy

Only post photos you have the right to share. Get people's okay before posting pictures that clearly show them, and never share someone's personal information without their permission. Be especially careful with photos of minors.

We care deeply about the safety of young people in our community. Any content that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits a minor is strictly prohibited and will be removed immediately, with the account permanently restricted and the matter reported to the proper authorities where required.

Play fair and play safe

  • Christ-centered sportsmanship. Play with honesty, humility, and respect for fellow players, organizers, referees, and the venue. Aggressive, abusive, or unsafe behavior may lead to removal.
  • Show up ready, respect the organizers' calls, and keep games safe.
  • Settle disputes calmly. Organizers have the final say on game-day decisions.
  • Respect the venue. Follow the host venue's rules and help keep the space clean.
  • Proper gear. Wear appropriate athletic attire and non-marking court shoes for indoor venues, and bring water.

Reservations and attendance

  • Confirmed slot required. Don't come to a game unless your reservation slot is confirmed. Walk-ins without a confirmed slot may not be accommodated.
  • No cancellations within 4 hours of game start. Cancel earlier so someone on the waitlist can take your slot.
  • No-shows. If you reserve and don't show up without canceling, you may be unable to join the next game until an organizer approves you.
  • Slots are non-transferable without informing an organizer, and fees should be settled on or before game day.

Health and liability

Basketball, pickleball, badminton, running, and related activities carry an inherent risk of injury. You take part at your own risk, and CCF Makati Sports, its organizers, leaders, and volunteers are not liable for injuries, accidents, or hospitalization arising from the runs, open plays, or any activity. Don't play if you're injured or unwell, consult a doctor for any medical condition, and tell an organizer if you feel unwell during play. You confirm this waiver at registration and each time you reserve a slot.

How we enforce this

Organizers may remove posts, hide content, or restrict accounts that break these guidelines, at their discretion. Serious or repeated violations can lead to removal from the community. We aim to be fair and to talk things through first whenever we can.
